Our beginning
The businesses that need the most support are usually the ones who have the least of it.

Raeform started from a simple belief: that small businesses, nonprofits, and purpose-driven founders deserve the same quality of support that bigger organizations take for granted. Not watered-down versions of it. The real thing.

It started with design — because that's where the passion was. But it quickly became clear that the businesses Trishia wanted to help needed more than visuals. They needed someone who could also handle the admin that was piling up, the operations that had no structure, the website that had been "almost done" for months, the workflows that only existed in one person's head.

So Raeform became the place where all of that gets handled. Branding, design, web development, automation, administrative support, staffing, consulting — built into one operation so clients don't have to coordinate five different people to move their business forward.

The work spans the Philippines and beyond, fully remote, with clients ranging from individual founders just getting started to established organizations that need to catch up with where they're headed. The projects look different. The standard doesn't change.
