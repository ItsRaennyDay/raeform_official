About RAEFORM

Built for the ones
doing work that matters.

Our beginning
The businesses that need the most support are usually the ones who have the least of it.

It started with a passion for design — and an even bigger passion for the people behind the work. Nonprofits doing real things for real communities. Small business owners putting everything on the line for something they believe in. Those were the people I wanted to be in the room with.

But somewhere along the way, I realized that what they needed wasn't just a logo or a website. They needed someone who could see the whole picture — the brand that wasn't landing, the admin that was eating their time, the systems that didn't exist yet, the vision that was stuck in their head with no clear way out.

That's why I built Raeform. Not just as a design studio, but as a place where all of it gets handled — branding, operations, web, automation, support — so the people doing meaningful work can actually focus on doing it.

I believe every brand should speak to who you are and what you stand for. That's the whole reason this exists.

— Trishia "Rae" Raymundo, Founder of RAEFORM

What we're here for
Mission, Vision
& Core Values
Mission

To give nonprofits, small businesses, and purpose-driven organizations the operational, creative, and strategic support they need to do their best work — without having to figure it all out alone.

Vision

A world where the size of an organization doesn't determine the quality of support it has access to — where the businesses doing the most meaningful work are also the best equipped to grow.
